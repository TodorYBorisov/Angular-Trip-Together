To learn how Trip Together works, just click on the button below.

  1. Register in the app, it takes less than a minute.
  2. Check your next journey, if you can't find it, create your own.
  3. If you find it join the trip and meet your trip companions.
  4. You can see all your created trips on your profile page as well.
  5. You can search for a trip based on the city name.
